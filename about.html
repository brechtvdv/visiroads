About

Visi Roads is a start-up in making publicity on the road. Companies can sell or buy "white" space on trucks. By the use of our custom made heatmap, companies can choose the region they want to have publicity.

This is a fictional company for a school project.

Who we are

Visi Roads is a startup project for University Ghent.

What We Do

We provide truck side advertising.

Why Choose Us?

  • Publishers: Easy to work with our platform: set your criteria, select Easy to target specific regions by our heatmap feature.

  • Advertisers: Increase your profit margins by allowing publicity on your truck. We take care of the rest.
